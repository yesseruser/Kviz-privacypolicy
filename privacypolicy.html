kvíz! privacy policy

This policy explains how kvíz! collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Information We Collect

The app may collect JSON documents that you voluntarily provide to play or edit using it. The content of the documents you provide is stored on your device and is not stored online.

Use of Your Information

The app may use your personal information to automatically fill the name boxes. The app may save your name into its JSON documents, but it will not store it online.

Protection of Your Information

The information stored in kvíz! is currently not being encrypted in any way. It is stored locally on your device and is not stored online.

Your Choices

You may choose not to import JSON documents from your device.

Changes to Our Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us at yesseruseryt@gmail.com.
